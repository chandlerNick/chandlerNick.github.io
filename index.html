About Me

Hey there! I'm Nick, a recent graduate from Western Washington University (WWU) with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and minors in both Mathematics and Statistics. I'm currently hoping to go to graduate school in Germany or Austria for Data Science, Cybersecurity, or AI. In the meantime I'm back home in Washington, just outside of Seattle, making the most of this Summer.

More About Me

During my time at WWU, I decided that I really like this technology and logic stuff. I worked as a math tutor for a couple years and engaged with academic research in the Mathematics department and Computer Science department there. I hope to continue growing in that realm by attending graduate school, obtaining a Mater of Science and potentially a PhD if it seems right.

When I'm not reading about technology, programming, doing math, or making things, I enjoy physical activity. I have done several bike rides of distances over 100 miles! I also ran my first marathon in September 2023! A goal I have this Summer is to summit Mt. Adams and Mt. St. Helens, two of Washington's five volcanoes.
